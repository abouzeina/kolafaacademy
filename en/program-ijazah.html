Quranic Ijazah Program

An advanced path to obtain a certified Ijazah in Quranic recitation or memorization with a connected Sanad reaching the Prophet (PBUH).

Book Your Assessment via WhatsApp

About the Program

The Ijazah is a testimony from a qualified teacher that the student has mastered the recitation or memorization of the Quran and is now eligible to teach others. This program is for those who have already completed the memorization of the Quran or wish to perfect their recitation to the highest standard of excellence (Itqan).

Why Choose this Program

Our Ijazah program is led by scholars who hold high Sanads in the ten Qira'at. We ensure a rigorous and meticulous review process where every letter and rule of Tajweed is scrutinized. Receiving an Ijazah from Kholafa Academy connects you to a long chain of scholars dedicated to preserving the Book of Allah.

What You Will Learn

  • Mastering Articulation Points (Makharij)
  • Deep Dive into Tajweed Theory and Practice
  • Reciting the Entire Quran to a Certified Sheikh
  • Understanding the Chain of Narration (Sanad)
  • Methods of Teaching and Passing the Ijazah
